Hi, astronomer's think I'm the new and exciting Planet 9!

In 2016, researchers proposed the possible existence of a ninth planet, for now dubbed "Planet Nine" or Planet X. The planet is estimated to be about 10 times the mass of Earth and to orbit the sun between 300 and 1,000 times farther than the orbit of the Earth. Since the planet has not been confirmed with physical evidence yet, scientists inferred its existence by its gravitational effects on other objects in the Kuiper Belt, a region at the fringe of the solar system that is home to icy rocks left over from the birth of the solar system.
